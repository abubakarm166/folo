Welcome to PnLBnB, accounting for short term rental hosts

A simple, intuitive way to keep track of revenue and expenses, built specifically for Airbnb and VRBO users.

Accounting Automation

Made Specifically for Short Term Rental Hosts

PnLBnB was created by hosts who were sick of the lack of accounting software that took advantage of the unique short term rental needs. Stuck between manually entering booking data into spreadsheets or having to use Quickbooks or long term rental software, We created PnLBnB to automate these tasks and provide graphical insights into how our properties are performing.
